About Our NGO

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to creating a positive impact in society through education, environmental sustainability, women empowerment, animal welfare, hunger relief, and youth development. The organization believes that every individual deserves equal opportunities and works tirelessly to build a better future through various community development initiatives.

Our Ongoing Projects

Project SEVA

Distribution of food, clothes, and basic necessities to underprivileged communities.

Project Bachpanshala

Educational support for children through learning programs, mentorship, and awareness initiatives.

Project JEEV

Dedicated to animal welfare through feeding drives, rescue support, and awareness campaigns.

Project UDAAN

Empowering women through skill development, education, and self-employment opportunities.

Project PRAKRITI

Environmental conservation through tree plantation drives and sustainability awareness programs.

Project VIKAS

Providing internship opportunities and skill enhancement programs for students and young professionals.

Social Impact

  • Supporting underprivileged communities through food and clothing drives.
  • Promoting quality education among children.
  • Encouraging environmental sustainability and green initiatives.
  • Empowering women through skill development programs.
  • Providing youth with practical industry experience through internships.
  • Improving animal welfare through rescue and feeding campaigns.
